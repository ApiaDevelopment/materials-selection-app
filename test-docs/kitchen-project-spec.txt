PROJECT SPECIFICATION - KITCHEN REMODEL
Project: Sturgeon Residence Kitchen Renovation
Address: 1402 Mink Trail
Project Type: Kitchen Remodel
Budget: $1,000

SCOPE OF WORK:

1. CABINETRY
   - Custom white shaker-style cabinets
   - Soft-close hinges and drawer slides
   - Crown molding finish
   - Estimated: 18 linear feet upper cabinets, 22 linear feet lower cabinets

2. COUNTERTOPS
   - Material: Quartz composite
   - Color: White with gray veining
   - Edge profile: Eased edge
   - Backsplash: 4-inch quartz backsplash matching countertops
   - Total square footage: 45 sq ft

3. APPLIANCES
   - Refrigerator: Stainless steel, 36" French door
   - Range: Gas, 30" with convection oven
   - Dishwasher: Stainless steel, quiet operation (45dB or less)
   - Microwave: Over-range microwave with exhaust fan

4. FLOORING
   - Material: Luxury vinyl plank (LVP)
   - Color: Light oak finish
   - Waterproof rated
   - Total area: 180 sq ft

5. PLUMBING FIXTURES
   - Sink: Undermount stainless steel, 33" double bowl
   - Faucet: Pull-down spray, brushed nickel finish
   - Garbage disposal: 3/4 HP continuous feed

6. LIGHTING
   - Recessed LED lighting: 6 fixtures
   - Under-cabinet LED strip lighting
   - Pendant lights over island: 3 fixtures, brushed nickel

7. ELECTRICAL
   - GFCI outlets: 4 locations
   - Dedicated circuit for refrigerator
   - Dedicated circuit for range

TIMELINE:
Estimated completion: 4-6 weeks from start date

SPECIAL NOTES:
- All work must comply with local building codes
- Permits required for electrical and plumbing work
- Contractor to protect existing hardwood floors in adjacent rooms during construction
